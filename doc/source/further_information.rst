Other resources:

* `Debian WSClean tracker <https://tracker.debian.org/pkg/wsclean>`_

  Lists the current status of the WSClean Debian package.

* `LOFAR Imaging Cookbook <https://support.astron.nl/LOFARImagingCookbook>`_

  Provides LOFAR-specific info.
  
* `Offringa et al. 2014 <http://arxiv.org/abs/1407.1943>`_

  The original WSClean paper.
  
* `Offringa & Smirnov 2017 <http://arxiv.org/abs/1706.06786>`_

  The MF multi-scale clean & auto-masking algorithms.
  
* `Van der Tol, Veenboer & Offringa (2018) <https://arxiv.org/abs/1909.07226>`_

  IDG overview paper.
  
* `Veenboer, Petschow & Romein (2018) <https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/7967145>`_

  IDG on Graphics Processors.
  
* `Offringa et al. (2019) <https://arxiv.org/abs/1908.11232>`_
  
  Assessment of gridding accuracy for 21-cm Epoch of Reionization experiments.
  
* `Ceccotti et al. (2023) <https://doi.org/10.1093/mnras/stad2465>`_

  Introduces the forced-spectrum deconvolution method
